Mbuyimayi, Democratic Republic of Congo

Mbujimayi, formerly known as Bakwanga, is a major city in central Democratic Republic of the Congo and the capital of Kasaï-Oriental province. It developed rapidly from the 1930s due to industrial diamond mining. Today, it remains an important mining and economic center while gradually diversifying into services and infrastructure development.
